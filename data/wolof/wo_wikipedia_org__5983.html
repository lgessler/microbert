Wommat wu mbëj

Wommat wu mbëj ab tur bees jox benn feeñteb jëmm bi la ngir mu teewal nangu gu ab wommatukaay dawaanu mbëj jàll ko. Wommat wi dafa aju ci wommatiin wi. Wommat wi G mooy safaanu ndëgërlu gi R.

G = R − 1 {\displaystyle G=R^{-1}\;}

Bennanu natt bu wommat wi mooy siemens (s).

1 S = 1 Ω − 1 {\displaystyle 1\mathrm {\ S} =1\mathrm {\ \Omega } ^{-1}}

Ω {\displaystyle \Omega } di ohm moom lañuy nattee ndëgërlu gu mbëj gi.
