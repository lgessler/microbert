Diiwaanu Ndakaaru, benn la ci fukk ak juroom-ñenti diiwaan yu Senegaal yi. Moo ci gën a tuuti, ci yaatuwaay, waaye moo ci gën a bari way-dëkk. Yaatuwaayam toll ci 550 km²
