Shotlandiye

Shotlandiye bolsa Yawropa jaylaxkan bir dulat. Uning paytahti bolsa Idinburg, ang qong xahri bolsa Glasgow. Dulat tili bolsa Shotlandiye gaél tili. Azirki Zungtung Alex Salmond. Azirki bash wezir Gordon Brown. Yar maydani 78 ming 772 kuwadirat kilometir, nopusi 5 million 144 ming, bularning assaslik dini Protéstant dini.
