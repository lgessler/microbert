Reciprocity

Indian Government has not drafted any laws to mandate reciprocity in visa agreements with other countries. While a very small number bilateral agreements have ended in reciprocity in visa arrangements, a large number of visa relationships continue to be highly skewed on one side or the other. This has highly restricted travelling freedom for Indian citizens outside India as well as restrictions on travel from countries which have traditionally welcomed Indian citizens with liberal visa policies.
